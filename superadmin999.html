Bond explained
The bond amount is set by you when adding the traveler — based on the stakes of their trip.
When a planner claims a set with bond plans, a bond is held from their payout for those specific plans.
Deliver on time → bond returned in full with higher commission.
Miss deadline → bond forfeited to protect the traveler and your brand.
Set rules
A set can hold 1–15 travelers — fill what you need, empty slots are ignored.
Planners claim the full set — no cherry-picking individual travelers from inside it.
Payout only released when all plans in the set are approved.
