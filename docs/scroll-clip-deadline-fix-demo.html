Today's 3 fixes — feel the before / after

All three are live in production. Each section below reproduces the real mechanism — try them.

1 The scroll that fought back（スクロールが戻される）

The editor re-renders constantly (route results arriving, times re-snapping). After every pass it "restored" the scroll position — yanking the planner back. Try to scroll both boxes down. A red flash = a re-render pass just happened.

re-render → scroll restored

✓ AFTER humans win, layout jumps still fixed

2 The invisible ceiling（重い日の下が切れる）

Day sections have an expand/collapse animation with a maximum height. A heavy day (Plan 5 · Day 3: 7 stops) grew taller than the maximum — everything below it was clipped out of existence. Scrolling could never reach it, on any device.

✕ BEFORE max-height:9999px + overflow:hidden

Scroll to the bottom — the summary is cut mid-card, the add button simply doesn't exist.

— clipped here: below this line nothing renders —

✓ AFTER ceiling raised far beyond any real day

Same content — bottom fully reachable: full summary + add button.

3 The deadline that lied（期限当日の朝9時に「1日遅延」）

Deadline = July 8, valid until 23:59 (the submit lock agrees, CS says so too). Drag through time and watch both chips. The old chip parsed the date as 09:00 Japan time and rounded any lateness up to a full day.
