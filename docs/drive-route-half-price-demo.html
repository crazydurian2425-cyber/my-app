Does anything on the platform change?

  • SAME Distance (km) — identical, same road
  • SAME Cost/fare — computed from the km, so identical
  • SAME Route map line, time-chain, autosave, snapshots, submit rules — all consume the result exactly as today
  • SAME Transit & walking legs — untouched (transit keeps its departure time for train schedules)
  • ±few min Drive duration: today = predicted traffic at that exact minute (17 min); proposed = Google's typical drive time (15 min). For trips booked weeks ahead, both are estimates — and the planner's exact-value override still beats both.
Why Google prices it this way: the expensive option asks Google to simulate live/predicted traffic for one exact departure minute — valuable for a dispatch app sending a car right now, much less valuable for an itinerary happening in 3 weeks. The cheap option answers the same "how far, how long, which road" question with typical drive times. The change is literally one line in the request — nothing else in the platform moves.
