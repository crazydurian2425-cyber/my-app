1 Opening a plan re-cascades it — and saves the new times 💾 auto-saved

Why it exists: the refresh runs on open, so the whole day snaps to the clean cascade — that's what makes “8:35” stick on reopen. The catch: any times you'd hand-set with gaps get overwritten the next time the plan opens.

Why it happens: the route cache buckets transit legs into 3-hour bands (9:00 / 12:00 / 15:00 …). Moving a departure from 9:00 to 8:50 (the 5-min fix) crosses a band, so the saved route key no longer matches → one fresh fetch, then it re-caches.
