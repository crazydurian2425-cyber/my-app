Two separate problems — and they are NOT the same severity

A · TRUE BUG Activity 09:45 starts BEFORE the taxi arrives at 09:55. Time runs backwards — physically impossible. Mechanism: _enforceTimeChain asks _transportArrivalMin for the taxi's arrival. That function reads the duration out of the visible grid cell ("10 min"). If the route wasn't resolved when the chain last ran, it returns null, and the chain deliberately skips the pair rather than clobbering — so the activity keeps its seeded 09:45 (= the taxi's departure). The route later resolves and the grid shows “10 min”, but no full chain pass re-runs on this day, so the wrong time survives on screen. This must be fixed.

B · DESIGN QUESTION Checkout 09:20 sits 50 min after the 08:30 day-start. The morning line is a locked bookend, and _enforceTimeChain intentionally skips locked bookends (if (a.is-locked) continue), so the first real stop is never pulled up to the day-start — it keeps its own saved 09:20. That gap may be intentional slack (breakfast at the hotel between 08:30 and checkout) rather than a bug. That's why the “Tight” toggle is a choice, not a default.

Why the taxi shows 09:45 (not 09:35) — it's correct, just surprising

Checkout 09:20 + 15 min stay = 09:35. But _suggestTransportStart rounds the departure up to the next 15-minute mark (Math.ceil(total/15)*15) → 09:45. Arrival = 09:45 + 10 min = 09:55. So the taxi block itself is self-consistent; only the activity below it failed to follow the arrival.
