Submit rules — v2 (wording + end-time gate)

Two refinements on top of the per-day structure rule: (1) "at least N" wording, and (2) each day must end within ±15 min of its suggested end before submit.

1 · "At least" wording (your point)

Counts now read as a minimum floor, not a fixed target — so planners know 2 is the least, not the goal (full days recommend 2–3).

Before
Day 2 needs: 2 more attractions (0/2), 2 more meals (0/2)
After (EN)
Day 2 needs: at least 2 attractions (have 0), at least 2 meals (have 0)
Before
1日目（到着日）に追加が必要：見どころあと2件（0/2）…
After (JA)
2日目に必要：見どころ2件以上（現在0件）、食事2件以上（現在0件）

2 · End-of-day timing gate (new)

Every plan already shows a suggested end time from the traveler's pace (early ≈ 9:00 pm · standard ≈ 10:30 pm · late ≈ 11:30 pm), and a ⚠ too early / ⚠ too late badge when the day's last stop is more than 15 min off it. Now that same ±15 min check also blocks submit. Departure day uses its own cut-off: departure time − airport buffer.

3 · The warnings a planner sees

    4 · Two things to confirm

    Q1 — Arrival day (Day 1) timing: the traveler lands mid-afternoon (~2 pm), so Day 1 is naturally short. Should the ±15 end-time rule apply to the arrival day too, or only to full days + departure day? (My lean: skip arrival day's end-time — only enforce its structure: 2 attractions + 1 meal + check-in.)

    Q2 — Departure day, ending early: some travelers have an early flight, so the planner physically can't fill the morning up to the cut-off. Do you want departure day to block both too-early and too-late, or only too-late (overshooting the leave-for-airport cut-off, which risks the flight)? (My lean: only block too-late on departure day — allow an early finish for early flights.)

    Reminder: this stacks on the structure rule. A day must have the right pieces (≥ attractions / meals / check-in) and end on time before it passes. Shown one day at a time (jumps + flashes that day), same as now.
