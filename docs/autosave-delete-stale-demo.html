📉 Demo — why 稲葉さん's Day 1 disappeared

The autosave doesn't just "save changes." Every time it runs it does this: take whatever is on the screen right now, put that in the database, and DELETE everything in the database that isn't on the screen. That's fine when the screen is complete. It's a disaster when the screen is momentarily incomplete. Pick a scenario and press ▶ Run autosave.
