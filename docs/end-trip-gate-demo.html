New submit rule: every day must be “closed”

A plan can't be submitted until each day ends with its ride out — the planner must press the end button so the day doesn't just stop at a venue.

1 · The rule

Day typeMust pressWhat it adds
Arrival + full days🌙 1日を終える · ホテルに戻るa transport leg back to the hotel
Departure day (last)✈️ 旅行を終える · {空港/駅}へ移動the final ride to the airport / station

How it's detected: the day's last item is a transport leg (the closing ride). If the day ends on a stop (sightseeing / restaurant), the button wasn't pressed → blocked.
