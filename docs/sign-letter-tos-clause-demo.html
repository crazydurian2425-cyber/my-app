2 things to decide before this can go in:
1. The 利用規約 has to actually exist as a real, readable document (a page/PDF the planner can open) — the clause links to it. Right now there's no 利用規約 URL in the letter. Do you have one to link to?
2. What it does: this clause makes the planner agree to whatever the 利用規約 contains. If the bond / forfeiture rules live in the 利用規約, signing this is what binds them to those rules — so the 利用規約's wording matters as much as this clause.
