3.Your role as a Planner

Planners work with Journey Junction as independent contractors, not employees. You choose which sets to accept from your dashboard and are responsible for delivering itineraries that meet our published quality standards.

Obligations

  • Quality. Each itinerary must be original, factually accurate, and tailored to the traveler's brief.
  • Deadlines. Complete sets by the deadline shown on your dashboard. If you can't, request an extension before it lapses.

  • Originality. You warrant that itineraries, images, and notes you upload are your own work or properly licensed for use.

4.Commission and payouts

Each set's commission is displayed on your dashboard before you claim it. Payouts are made in Japanese Yen (¥) via bank transfer or PayPay, depending on the payout method you set in your profile.

Payout timing

Commission is released after all 15 plans in a set are reviewed and approved by admin. Standard release time is within 24 hours of final approval, subject to banking-day delays.

Weekly salary

Planners who complete at least 1 set in a rolling 7-day cycle are paid a ¥50,000 weekly salary, credited to their wallet after the cycle ends. The first cycle begins on the date the planner completes their first-ever set; subsequent cycles run as consecutive 7-day windows anchored to that same date. The salary is in addition to per-plan commission and bond refunds. Cycles in which no set is completed do not qualify.

Wallet protection buffer

Journey Junction may hold a protection buffer in your wallet to cover post-approval adjustments (disputed plans, reversals, bond corrections) without clawing back from your bank. The buffer amount, if any, is set by the platform and shown in your Wallet at any time. Anything earned above the buffer is fully withdrawable. We may revise the buffer amount from time to time at our discretion.

Large-payout security deposit

To protect the platform and travelers against fraud, chargebacks, and coordinated account manipulation, any withdrawal of ¥1,000,000 or more requires a refundable Platform Usage Deposit starting at ¥600,000 — scaled to the withdrawal amount and risk profile — held in Journey Junction escrow.

The deposit is released within one business day after the withdrawal clears, provided no traveler disputes, chargebacks, or compliance reviews are open against your plans. The deposit earns no interest and is returned in full to your registered bank account on release.

The deposit may be forfeited in whole or in part if: (a) confirmed fraud, identity misrepresentation, or account manipulation is found; (b) a chargeback or dispute is resolved against Journey Junction before the deposit is released; or (c) you withdraw funds and attempt to close the account to evade a pending investigation.

5.Commitment bonds

If you miss a deadline without an approved extension, or materially fail to deliver a bond-secured plan, the bond may be forfeited in whole or in part to protect the traveler.
