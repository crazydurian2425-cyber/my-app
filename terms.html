4.Commission and payouts

Each set's commission is displayed on your dashboard before you claim it. Payouts are made in Japanese yen (¥) via bank transfer, depending on the payout method you set in your profile.

Payout timing

Commission is released after all 15 plans in a set are reviewed and approved by admin. Standard release time is within 24 hours of final approval, subject to banking-day delays.

Performance bonus

Planners who complete and receive approval for at least 2 sets in a 7-day cycle are awarded a ¥150,000 performance bonus, credited to their wallet after the cycle ends. The first cycle begins on the date the planner completes their first-ever set; subsequent cycles run as consecutive 7-day windows anchored to that same date. The bonus is in addition to per-plan commission, and is not a guaranteed salary. Cycles in which fewer than 2 sets are completed and approved do not qualify.

Wallet protection buffer

Journey Junction may hold a protection buffer in your wallet to cover post-approval adjustments (disputed plans, reversals) without clawing back from your bank. The buffer amount, if any, is set by the platform and shown in your Wallet at any time. Anything earned above the buffer is fully withdrawable. We may revise the buffer amount from time to time at our discretion.
