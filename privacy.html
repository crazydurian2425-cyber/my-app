How we handle planner information

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026 · Operated for the Japan market

1.Summary

Journey Junction ("we") operates a platform connecting travelers visiting Japan with inbound-Japan travel planners. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you register as a planner and use the service, the purposes for collection, and how it is managed. It applies to use of the planner dashboard, support chat, and related services.

2.Data we collect

WhatWhy
Name, email, phoneAccount creation, login, support, and reward-payout confirmation.
Region of residence, languages, areas of expertiseMatching planners to suitable traveler briefs.
Payment-method informationPaying rewards to planners.
Itinerary content (text, images you upload)Providing the itinerary created by the planner to the intended traveler.
Support-chat messages & attachmentsResponding to inquiries and keeping necessary records.
Dashboard activity logsPreventing misuse, improving the service, and checking for system issues.

3.How we use it

  • To run the account — authenticating the planner, displaying assigned sets, and processing reward payouts.
  • To assign work — when assigning sets, admin reviews the languages and areas of expertise listed by the planner.
  • To provide itineraries — travelers who requested a set can view the approved itinerary. Where applicable, the planner display name may be shown as the author.
  • To support and safeguard the platform — we may review chat logs as needed to respond to inquiries, check for bugs, or prevent misuse.

We do not sell the personal information of planners to third parties and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

4.Who we share with

  • Travelers — may see the planner display name, avatar, and the itinerary content submitted for their trip. They do not see email addresses, phone numbers, or payment-method information.
  • Supabase (database + authentication service) — an external service we use to store profile information and itineraries. Data is managed under appropriate contracts and safeguards.
  • Bank or payment service provider — handles payment-method information and amounts to the extent needed to pay rewards.
  • Tax authorities and law enforcement — if required by Japanese law.

Where an external service provider processes data outside Japan, we handle it with appropriate safeguards in place.

5.How long we keep it

Account information: kept while the planner account is active. After account deletion, we remove personally identifiable information within a reasonable period. However, records needed for legal compliance, tax, accounting, or dispute handling may be retained for a limited period to the extent permitted by law.

Itinerary content: kept for as long as the traveler can access the itinerary and as needed for service provision and recordkeeping, then archived or deleted.

Support messages: kept for as long as needed to respond to inquiries, maintain quality, and prevent misuse.

6.Planner rights

Under applicable personal information protection laws, planners have the right to:

  • Confirm the personal information we hold about you — request confirmation via support chat.
  • Correct inaccurate information — many fields are editable directly from your Profile.
  • Delete your account and associated information — contact support. Records required by law or for business needs may be retained for a limited period.
  • Withdraw consent for optional use of information at any time.
  • Inquiries & complaints — if you have questions or concerns about how we handle personal information, please contact support first. Where necessary, you may also consult the relevant authority.

7.Cookies & local storage

We use browser localStorage to keep you signed in and to save preferences (language, notification toggles, etc.). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies. Clearing your browser storage may sign you out.

8.Security

We use reasonable security measures including encrypted transport (HTTPS), access-permission management, appropriate use of external services, and review of access logs. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure. If we become aware of an incident that may affect personal information, we will take the necessary steps in accordance with applicable laws.

9.Children

Journey Junction is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that someone under 18 has created an account, we will take prompt action.

10.Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We may announce material changes on the dashboard. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the current version.

Privacy contact

To confirm, correct, or delete your personal information, or for any privacy question, reach us via in-app support chat or hello@thejourneyjunction.co.uk.
