How we handle your data

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026 · Operated for the Japan market (APPI applies)

1.Summary

Journey Junction ("we") operates a planner marketplace connecting itinerary writers with travelers visiting Japan. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you register as a Planner, why we need it, and how you can control it. It applies to your use of the planner dashboard, support chat, and related services.

2.Data we collect

WhatWhy
Name, email, phoneAccount creation, login, support contact, payout confirmations.
City, languages, specialtiesMatching you to suitable traveler briefs.
Bank name, account number, account holder namePaying commissions and refundable bonds into your account.
Itinerary content (text, photos you upload)Delivering the plan to the traveler you created it for.
Support-chat messages & attachmentsAnswering your questions and keeping a record for audit.
Dashboard activity logsFraud prevention, performance metrics, debugging.

3.How we use it

  • To run your account — authenticating you, displaying sets assigned to you, processing payouts.
  • To match you with work — admin reviews your languages and specialties when assigning sets.
  • To deliver itineraries — travelers who commissioned a set see the approved plan with your first name attached as the author.
  • To support and protect the platform — we review chat logs only when a dispute, bug, or abuse report arises.

We do not sell your data to third parties and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

4.Who we share with

  • Travelers — see your first name, avatar, and the itinerary content you submit for their trip. They do not see your email, phone, or bank details.
  • Supabase (database + auth hosting) — our processor for storing profile data and itineraries. Data is hosted on their infrastructure under their DPA.
  • Our bank / payment processor — receives only the minimum needed to transfer commission (account name, number, bank name, amount).
  • Tax authorities and law enforcement — if required by Japanese law.

No data is transferred outside Japan except to the extent our processors (e.g. Supabase) operate cross-border under appropriate safeguards.

5.How long we keep it

Account data: kept while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove personal identifiers within 30 days, but may keep audit logs and payout records for 7 years to meet Japanese tax and accounting law.

Itinerary content: kept for as long as the traveler has access to the plan (typically the duration of their trip plus 180 days), then archived.

Support messages: kept for 2 years for audit, then deleted.

6.Your rights

Under Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) and equivalent international frameworks, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you — request a copy via support chat.
  • Correct inaccurate data — most fields are editable directly from your Profile.
  • Delete your account and associated data — contact support; subject to the retention periods in §5.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential processing at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with Japan's data protection authority, the Personal Information Protection Commission (PPC / 個人情報保護委員会), if you believe we've mishandled your data.

7.Cookies & local storage

We use browser localStorage to keep you signed in and remember preferences (language, notification toggles). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies. Clearing your browser storage will sign you out.

8.Security

We use industry-standard security measures: encrypted transport (HTTPS), encrypted-at-rest database storage via Supabase, role-based access so only authorised staff see bank details, and regular review of access logs. No system is 100% secure; if we become aware of a breach affecting your data we will notify you and report to the Personal Information Protection Commission (PPC) promptly, as required by the APPI.

9.Children

Journey Junction is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that an under-18 user has created an account we will delete it promptly.

10.Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on your dashboard at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.

Privacy contact

For data requests, deletion, or any privacy question, reach us via in-app support chat or email hello@thejourneyjunction.co.uk.
