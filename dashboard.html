    Welcome to Journey Junction
    A quick look at what you can do here.
    What is Journey Junction
    Journey Junction is a curated marketplace connecting travelers across France with skilled local planners. You craft the itineraries, travelers enjoy the trip, and we handle the logistics behind the scenes.
    Your role as a planner
    You'll design personalized itineraries based on each traveler's brief, their pace, preferences, and must-sees. Every plan you build shapes someone's real journey across France.
    Tools you'll use
    Everything lives in one dashboard. Claim new assignments, track your active sets, build day-by-day itineraries, chat with support, and monitor your wallet, all in one place.
    We've got your back
    Support is one tap away for questions, revisions, or payment help. Admins review your work carefully and the planner community is here to share tips and encouragement whenever you need it.

    Why a bond is required
    This plan is a premium booking — usually a corporate client, large group, or high-value traveler. Delivery is time-sensitive: a late or missing itinerary can cause them to lose flights, hotels, or paid bookings, and reflects directly on Journey Junction.
    The bond ensures you're committed to finishing the set on schedule. Think of it as a deposit that secures your seat on the job.
    What you should know
    • ✓Fully refundable — returned to you in full once all 15 plans in the set are submitted and approved.
    • ✓Your commission is paid separately — the bond does not reduce your earnings.
    • ✓Only forfeited if the set is abandoned or plans are submitted past the deadline without notice.
