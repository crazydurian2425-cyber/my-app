Frequently asked
How do payouts work?+
You get paid when your full set (15 plans) is reviewed and approved by admin. Your commission plus any bond deposits you paid are released together to your registered bank account, usually within 1hr of approval.
Is there a weekly salary?+
Yes — €50,000 each rolling 7-day cycle, as long as you complete at least 1 set in that cycle. Your first cycle starts the day you complete your first-ever set; the next cycle starts 7 days later, and so on. The salary is credited to your wallet after each cycle ends, on top of per-plan commission. Cycles with zero completed sets don't qualify.
What's a bond plan?+
Premium bookings (corporate, VIP, or large groups) require a refundable bond to secure the plan. The bond is returned in full together with your commission once all 15 plans are approved.
Admin requested a revision — now what?+
Open Dashboard — the "Revisions requested" card at the top shows the admin's notes. Click "Fix now" to reopen the itinerary form, apply the changes, and submit again.
Can I skip a plan I'm stuck on?+
Plans go in order — you can't skip. If a plan's brief doesn't have enough info, chat us here with the plan number and we'll help you unblock.
How long does admin review take?+
Usually within 1hr of your last plan being submitted. If it's been longer, chat us and we'll nudge the reviewer.
Can I work on multiple sets at once?+
Not right now — one active set at a time. Once your current set is paid out, a new set can be assigned to you.
I missed my deadline. What happens?+
Message us before the deadline — we can often extend it. If you've ghosted the set, admin may reassign it and any bond on that set may be forfeited.
Tips for faster help
Include your plan number and traveler name when asking about a specific itinerary.
Attach screenshots (clip icon) for payment proofs or bugs — much faster than describing.
One topic per message — splits are easier for us to track.
